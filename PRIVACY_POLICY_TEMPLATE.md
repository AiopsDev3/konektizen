# Privacy Policy for Konektizen

**Last updated:** January 14, 2026

Konektizen ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

We utilize Facebook Login for authentication. When you log in with Facebook, we collect the following public profile information:

- **Name**: To identify you within the application.
- **Email Address**: For account verification and communication.
- **Profile Picture**: To display on your user profile.

We also collect the following permission-based data to facilitate incident reporting:

- **Location**: To pinpoint the location of incidents you report.
- **Camera/Photos**: To allow you to attach evidence to your reports.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate your identity.
- Process and verify incident reports submitted to Local Government Units (LGUs).
- Facilitate emergency responses.
- Display your profile within the app.
