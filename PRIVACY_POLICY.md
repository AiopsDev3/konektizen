# Privacy Policy for KONEKTIZEN

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-13

## 1. Introduction

KONEKTIZEN ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

We verify your identity and facilitate civic engagement. To do this, we collect:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number (optional).
- **Identity Information:** Government ID images (for verification purposes only).
- **Location Data:** Real-time GPS location (for emergency SOS reporting and verify report location).
- **Content:** Incident reports, photos, and videos you upload.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Verification:** enhancing safety by verifying user identities via OCR.
- **Emergency Response:** Sharing your location with responders during SOS alerts.
- **Civic Reporting:** displaying your reports to the community and local authorities.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

- **Authorities:** Verification of reports or emergency response.
- **Service Providers:** Third-party services (like AI analysis or map providers) under strict confidentiality.

## 5. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## 6. Contact Us

For questions about this policy, please contact us at: support@konektizen.com
